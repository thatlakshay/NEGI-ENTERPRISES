About Negi Enterprises

Bringing Color and Precision to Mohali & Chandigarh

For years, Negi Enterprises has been the trusted backbone for local businesses, startups, schools, shop owners, and corporate groups seeking premium design and print materials. We combine experienced graphic design specialists with heavy-duty commercial offset, digital flex, and vinyl printer systems to deliver outstanding visual impact.

Whether you require a single high-quality shop banner, specialized wedding cards, or bulk pamphlet printing for a wide-spread local campaign, we bring the same level of precision and quality check to every job.
